MY PORTFOLIO

I love to cook, not only for myself, but also for others. My sister is always teaching me new recipes, which results not only in delicious dishes, but also in enjoyable moments. I really enjoy cooking dishes that I like to eat. At least later I'll know what to cook if I'm living on my own.

Here, I'm relaxing at an exhibition in Tokyo, doing all sorts of things. I love discovering new places. My ultimate dream is to go to Japan one day, so this day was really crazy.

May I introduce my cat Tarzan, who has been a faithful friend for the past three years. He quickly became my source of support, always by my side when I was facing difficulties. Tarzan loves to play and loves to crash on my bed at night for a cozy nap.

This image represents me. I'm a person who likes to be left alone in peace and quiet. I'm nice to my friends. I have the character of a leader who knows how to manage groups. I'm allergic to dandelions, but that's okay.

Sunsets are one of the most beautiful things to see in the sky. Sometimes it changes color every time. Being with your friends with this view is beautiful, isn't it?

Here you can see the moon on my birthday last year. I'm really a night person, I love playing with friends in the evening. Just relaxing, feeling the peace and no one around to distract me. Nights are just my happy place.

I was thrilled when I graduated from high school. We had to go in front of a stage and take it in front of relatives and friends. I'm proud of myself and also of my colleagues.

Anime is my childhood, and I love watching all kinds. There's a lot of manga in the stores, and I always make a trip to the shelves. There's also made in asia, a manga/anime event. I've been there once, but I hope to go more often.

Who doesn't like to eat? I do. That's why I love to cook. I love discovering foods from every culture, but my favorite is Japanese food. Their rice, chicken, sauce, sushi, salad, noodles, soup. There are also Moroccan desserts that are very delicious.

Going out with my friends is what I love. Seeing my friends again is a pleasure. I'd like to get out with them more because I miss it. Doing activities together, eating together, playing games together.

Exploring new places excites me. Whenever I visit somewhere new, I remember the routes and spots like I have a built-in GPS in my head. It's not just about going places; it's about creating a mental map that brings each adventure to life. Discovering fresh locations keeps my curiosity alive and pushes me to seek out new adventures.

Art is beautiful to see, like going to a museum. The best museum I've explored is the Art & History Museum in Brussels. We have lots of artists in Belgium.
